Background and history

Purple Lake lanterns originated in ancient Fujian Minnan Lantern Festival. Minnan language "lights" and "Ding" homonym, said "Tim light" is "Tim Ding" meaning. During the first month on the streets of the streets on both sides of the residents in front of hanging lanterns to celebrate the New Year, peace and good fortune, people thriving. Each lantern covered with the whole street, the streets shine bright. Because the production process of each lantern is not the same, in different forms, with a strong ornamental, to attract people from all directions to watch, conventionally formed a Lantern Festival Lantern (this way is to light the lamp). Qing Dynasty Shunzhi, Kangxi years after the ancestors of immigrants after the purple lake, due to living scattered, can not achieve the street form of lanterns, so in the first month each household to carry lanterns together to form a variety of lanterns composed of long, Road and the various households, which is spread so far the purple lake lanterns, Minnan saying it as "light" (this light is the light of the people).
